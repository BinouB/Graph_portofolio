About me

I am a biologist based in Dakar, Senegal, with experience in molecular diagnostics and infectious diseases research. Currently, I contribute to the SEN‑B project at CHNU Fann, where I manage laboratory workflows, coordinate cross‑disciplinary teams, and co‑author scientific publications.

Curious about the intersection of biology and technology, I am exploring Generative AI to accelerate data analysis, automate routine tasks, and communicate science more effectively.

About the course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week, live online certification that teaches no‑code AI tools to streamline document creation, data analysis, and task automation.

  • Automate documents, spreadsheets, and data workflows
  • Organize knowledge and perform literature reviews with AI
  • Develop custom chatbots and Retrieval‑Augmented Generation systems
  • Create visuals and presentations for outreach
  • Build an end‑to‑end AI solution in a capstone project

Next cohort starts May 9 2025 • Live workshops on Fridays or Sundays • Tuition $250/mo for 2 months
