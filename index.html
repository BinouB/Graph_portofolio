About Me

I am a biologist based in Dakar, Senegal, with experience in molecular diagnostics and infectious diseases research. Currently, I contribute to the SEN‑B project at CHNU Fann, where I manage laboratory workflows, coordinate cross‑disciplinary teams, and co‑author scientific publications. My academic background spans a Professional Master's in Biological Analysis from IPE and a Bachelor's in Life and Earth Sciences from Cheikh Anta Diop University.

Curious about the intersection of biology and technology, I am now exploring Generative AI to accelerate data analysis, automate routine tasks, and communicate science more effectively.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week, live online certification that teaches no‑code AI tools to streamline document creation, data analysis, and task automation.

  • Automate documents, spreadsheets, and data workflows
  • Organize knowledge and perform literature reviews with AI
  • Develop custom chatbots and RAG systems
  • Create visuals and presentations for outreach
  • Build an end‑to‑end AI solution in a capstone project
